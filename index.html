What this
actually is

Crypto Analyzer is a static, client-side dashboard over the free CoinGecko API. It computes standard technical-analysis indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, Stochastic RSI, VWAP) and a simple linear-regression trend line — real math, clearly labelled, with no AI/ML models, no backend, and no accounts. Everything you see runs in your browser.
